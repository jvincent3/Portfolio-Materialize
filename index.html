Vincent and Rtyrolia

We are Developers with a rule of : You Dream it_ We Build it_

We are friends who knew each other for years and along with it we both love Web and Software Development. So we decided that it would be nice to start learning about it together on the go, support each other on any questions or ideas we might have and to get a lot of practice by actually building stuff, maintaining our projects and freelance!

Now, the boring stuff : The idea of studying Computer Science and IT came to us as soon as our informatics lessons started in school. It went further with one going to university to study Combined STEM ( Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics ) and another doing a lot for courses on Edx, Udacity and Udemy. Our interest in the subjects comes from our view of it as challenging, and because we’ve always loved challenges and it requires pure logic. We have a passion for developing websites, apps, or useful software - that's our way.
